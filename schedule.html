Program Schedule

CERTAIN is structured as a full-day workshop. The morning establishes the formal assurance of AI-enabled systems, with the Student Award Talks placed immediately after a keynote to give emerging researchers a central platform. Future Pitches then lead directly into posters and lunch, turning shared interests into conversations. The afternoon develops the complementary theme of AI for formal reasoning and concludes with an extended Guided Discussions session, converting the day's ideas into open problems and potential collaborations.
