Program Schedule

CERTAIN is structured as a full-day workshop with technical talks, student presentations, and built-in time for collaboration. The day combines keynotes, invited talks, student orals, posters over lunch, a Find Your Future Team session, and a guided brainstorming session designed to turn open problems into new research connections.
